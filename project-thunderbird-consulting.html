Thunderbird Consulting

Strategy Consultant Academic Project

Context

As part of the Master of Global Management program at Thunderbird, I participated in a consulting project to review deliverables and propose scalable operating guidelines for a client organization.

Tools

Process Mapping Strategic Analysis Presentation

Goals

  • Identify gaps in current operational processes.
  • Propose scalable guidelines for future growth.
  • Present actionable recommendations to stakeholders.

Approach

We conducted a thorough review of the client's existing documentation and deliverables. By interviewing key stakeholders and analyzing process flows, we identified bottlenecks and areas for improvement. We then developed a set of standardized operating guidelines designed to improve consistency and scalability.

Outcomes

  • Delivered a comprehensive gap analysis report.
  • Proposed scalable operating guidelines adopted by the client.
  • Received positive feedback for clarity and strategic insight.

Reflection

This project reinforced the value of an outside perspective. Often, internal teams are too close to the process to see the inefficiencies. Structured analysis can uncover simple changes that yield major results.
