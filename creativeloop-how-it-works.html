FAQ

Common questions

Do I have to do anything after setup?
No. Once you complete your brand setup, CreativeLoop runs automatically every week. You'll get a notification Monday morning when your content is ready to review and download.
Can I edit the content before I post it?
Yes. Every caption, image, and hashtag set is fully editable in your dashboard. You can also regenerate any individual piece if you want a different option.
Will my content look the same every week?
No. CreativeLoop rotates content themes, post formats, and angles each week to keep your feed varied and engaging. Your brand identity stays consistent, but the content stays fresh.
What platforms does the content work for?
CreativeLoop generates content optimised for Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and X (Twitter). You can specify which platforms you focus on during setup.
How long does setup take?
Under 5 minutes. Fill in your brand details, and your first week of content is generated and ready within minutes.
Do I need any design or copywriting skills?
None at all. CreativeLoop handles everything from image generation to caption writing. If you can answer a few questions about your brand, you're ready to go.
