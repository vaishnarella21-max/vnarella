Tradexa Technology

Project Coordinator Jun 2022 – Dec 2024

Context

Tradexa is a B2B technology platform for global trade. As a Project Coordinator, I was responsible for ensuring the timely delivery of technical projects and bridging the gap between client requirements and engineering execution.

Tools

Jira Slack Google Workspace

Goals

  • Ensure 100% on-time delivery for client projects.
  • Reduce ambiguity in requirements for the development team.
  • Streamline communication between stakeholders and technical teams.

Approach

I implemented a structured intake process for client requirements, ensuring all necessary details were captured before development began. I also established daily stand-ups and weekly progress reports to track project health and identify blockers early. By maintaining clear documentation, I reduced scope creep and rework.

Outcomes

  • 100% on-time delivery maintained across all assigned projects.
  • Reduced rework by clarifying requirements upfront.
  • Improved client satisfaction through transparent communication.

Reflection

This role taught me that successful project management is 80% communication. Clear expectations and proactive updates prevent most issues before they become critical.
