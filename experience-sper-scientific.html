Sper Scientific

Marketing & E-commerce Intern Jul 2025 – Aug 2025

Context

Sper Scientific sells scientific and industrial equipment. Listings across eBay and Walmart were inconsistent in structure, keywords, and visuals, reducing discoverability and conversion.

Tools

eBay Walmart Excel Google Workspace

Goals

  • Improve search visibility via consistent, keyword-aware titles.
  • Increase conversion by aligning imagery and key specs.
  • Create a repeatable process for future listing updates.

Approach

Audited existing listings for keyword usage and category placement, created a standard template for titles and bullets, and implemented batch updates in phases tied to seasonal promos.

Outcomes

  • 150+ listings standardized and updated.
  • Improved keyword relevance and category structure to support higher visibility.
  • Created a scalable workflow for future catalog updates.

Reflection

This experience highlighted the impact of structured data on e-commerce performance. Even small improvements in listing quality can drive significant gains in discoverability when applied at scale.
