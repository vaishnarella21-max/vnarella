Sper Scientific: Marketplace Listing Optimization

Standardized and optimized 150+ product listings for visibility and conversion across eBay and Walmart marketplaces.

Overview

Sper Scientific sells scientific and industrial equipment. Listings on eBay and Walmart were inconsistent in structure, keywords, and visuals, which hurt discoverability and conversion.

Challenge: Listings had grown over time with different formats and little standardization across a catalogue of hundreds of products.

Goals

  • Improve search visibility via consistent, keyword-aware titles.
  • Raise conversion by aligning imagery and key specs.
  • Create a repeatable process for future listing updates.

Approach

  1. Audit: Reviewed existing listings for keyword usage and category placement.
  2. Template: Defined a standard format for titles, bullets, and descriptions.
  3. Batch Updates: Applies the new guidelines to 150+ listings in phases.
  4. Campaign Tie-in: Coordinated updates with seasonal promos.

Outcomes

150+ Listings Fixed
SEO Visibility Up
Process Scalable
